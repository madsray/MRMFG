About Us

Since 1989 we have been producing high quality standard and custom stamped metal products. Mike Ray Manufacturing provides a high volume, low cost source for guage metal stampings to serve the plumbing industry.

First Class Facilities

At MRM we believe that having the best staff and facilities are paramount in producing the best products for our end users. We pride ourselves in producing those products for our customers and continue to provide the cleanest and safest facilities to ensure worker safety and high quality products.

Our Products

Nail Plates

Nail Plates

Nail Plates are used to protect piping running in the walls of many types of construction projects. Our 16 guage Nail Plates are stamped from 16 guage CG quality steel. Our Nail Plates can be manufactured in a wide range of sizes and hole punch patterns in order to accomodate any plumbing code requirement. We manufacture many common sizes and are able to accommodate custom requests as well.

Stud Guard

Stud Guard

Studguards are stamped from 16ga CQ quality steel. Studguards are manufactured to have two prongs at each end to allow them to be driven into wood construction material. Sudguards are typically 1.5" wide and 5" or 6" in length. Please call for custom sizes.

Stud Shoes

Stud Shoes

Stud shoes are manufactured from 18 gauge CQ quality milled steel. Stud shoes are manufactured to fit one, two, or three 2"x4" studs placed together along the 4" side. Stud shoes are used where it is necessary to notch a large opening in the stud.

Water Heater Pans

Water Heater Pans

Water Heater Pans are made from heady duty 28 gauge galvanized steel. Pans can be made to custom sizes and can be made without drain or with steel, CPVC, or PVC drains.

Water Heater Stands

Water Heating Stands

Water Heater Stands are made from 18 gauge galvanized steel. Stands feature heavy duty cross baces that are designed for water heaters that are 52 gallons or smaller
